Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

verified_user IT Act 2000 Compliance

In accordance with Section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the rules framed thereunder, this policy describes how we collect, store, transmit, and protect user data (Sensitive Personal Data or Information).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal credentials including registration email, secure scrypt hashed password tokens, and public transaction metadata (transaction ID, subscription plans, subscribed trader references). We do NOT store payment card numbers, UPI PINs, or net banking passwords; all transaction data is processed directly by Razorpay.

2. Data Security & Storage

Your passwords are scrypt hashed locally before saving into Supabase / local storage. We employ modern HTTPS protocols, rate limiters, and Helmet header integrations to block unauthorized script execution and SQL injections. Temporary MFA/TOTP codes are stored securely in database tables and removed instantly on validation.

3. Use of Personal Data

We use your email address to authenticate your dashboard profile and issue service announcements. Your subscription metrics are used to compile marketplace leaderboard rankings. We never sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third-party marketing brokers.

4. Cookie & LocalStorage Policies

We utilize standard LocalStorage key-value pairs (`saudaaAdminToken`, `saudaaToken`) to manage secure portal session states. This data is stored locally in your browser cache and is destroyed when your session is closed or when you click "Terminate Session".

5. Data Deletion Requests

Under Indian regulations, users retain the right to modify or request the deletion of their accounts. If you wish to delete your subscriber profile and associated chat/suggestion history, please submit a formal request to our grievance officer at `support@saudaa.com`.
